A new life?

Is life becoming monotonous? Doing the same job and living the same routine each day, time passing you by and looking back with no new memories or experiences. Why not try teaching abroad?

Thousands of schools in Cambodia are desperate for qualified Native English-speaking teachers and we can provide the qualifications and experience for you to achieve just that. You will have the opportunity to obtain a TEFL qualification and the essential experience to successfully educate young minds in a foreign land. Find yourself a rewarding job, where you are appreciated and each day consists of inspiring young students, who will grow to love you for it.

The school: Singapore Amicus International school is located near the south coast of Cambodia, with beautiful exotic beaches and picturesque islands. Spend the week honing your skills at our school and then break away at the weekend to paradise. We can provide all various types of support to make your experience go as smoothly and less daunting as possible. Try for a week or even a month to appease your curiosity and if you then find that this is the life you have been craving, our team will help you find a job in one of the many schools within Cambodia. Providing you with all the necessary documentation to make it happen.

The pay for teachers is more than adequate, in comparison with the living costs out here in Cambodia. You will have the opportunity to save an ample amount of money, if you decide teaching as your new career path. Visit prospects to learn more about the many benefits of living in Cambodia and you never know, you might even begin to learn a new language yourself.

Get Away

We provide support and relevant information, to help you gain confidence during your visit in Cambodia. You will get the opportunity to qualify the 120-hour TEFL course certification and obtain invaluable classroom experience with students who are learning English as their second language. With your spare time; visit Siem Reap to see Angkor Watt temple or relax on some of the most beautiful beached Islands in the World.

Half Break

We give you guidance for the entire month while you work and experience life as a foreign teacher. Showing you what life could possibly be, if you so choose. Providing information to help find a month accommodation to your liking and budget, easy ways for transportation and choices of food, including Western. Qualify your 120-hour TEFL course, guaranteed. With classroom experience around hours of your choosing. Giving you the freedom to experience all the wonders Cambodia has to offer.

Full Break

If you seem to like the what Cambodia has to offer and you decide to commit an entire year to this lifestyle. Then we will support you any way we can during the first month of you visit. After successfully completing your TEFL, gaining all the necessary qualifications and experience you need. Giving you the skills and confidence, so you can be a fully capable, self-functioning teacher for these children. We can then supply you with your year business visa and work permit, for an agreed upon price. Allowing you, with our support to apply and work for the many schools across Cambodia.

The School: Singapore Amicus International School

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to inspire the youth of Cambodia, to inspire our students towards excellence and maximize their potential. Our mission is to provide top quality education to our students through: Qualified Teachers – Selecting qualified teachers, and improving performance by feedback, appraisal and delivery. Curriculum – Implementing the Singaporean and Khmer curriculum for our full-time programmes. Chosen to give our students the best resources, from a country ranked number 1 in education.

Our Core Values

Since its inception, SAIS has been governed by its core values. They shape the culture and define the character of our school. They guide how we behave and make decisions. Imagination – We inquire with an open mind. We imagine a brighter future for ourselves and the people around us and seek to enrich the lives of those people. We care for each other, respect people as individuals and listen with understanding. Excellence – We strive to be the best that we can be. Courage to Change – We have the courage to receive truthful feedback from others, and change where change is required, bettering ourselves.

Our Culture

Culture is a set of shared beliefs and assumptions to move us towards our goals and governs our behaviour towards ourselves and towards our students, parents/guardians and others involved with the school. Our school’s motto is “transforming minds, inspiring excellence”. It is our belief that understanding and applying knowledge empowers the mind towards excellence in all areas of one’s life. With this in mind, we are committed to: U – Unique Q – Quality I – Integrity
